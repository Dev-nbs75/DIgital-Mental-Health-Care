About Us

Mentora was founded on the simple principle that quality mental healthcare should be accessible, understandable, and free from stigma. We are a team of passionate healthcare professionals, therapists, and technologists dedicated to creating a safe and supportive space for your mental wellness journey. By combining evidence-based therapeutic practices with intuitive technology, we empower you to stay close to your mind and build a healthier, more fulfilling life.
